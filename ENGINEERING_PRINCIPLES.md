# 🏛 Zyloq AI Engineering Principles

1. Understand before you build.
2. Learn from first principles.
3. Keep solutions simple and maintainable.
4. Measure before making claims.
5. Build for production from day one.
6. Automate repetitive work.
7. Document decisions, not only code.
8. Learn in public and teach others.
9. Security and privacy by default.
10. Never stop learning.
